Stage 1

Gonorrhea

A sexually transmitted bacterial infection that, if untreated, may cause infertility.




final stage

Regular screening can help detect instances when an infection is present
despite having no symptoms. Symptoms include painful urination and abnormal
discharge from the penis or vagina. Men may experience testicular pain and
women may experience pain in the lower stomach.
In some cases, gonorrhoea has no symptoms.




TREATMENT & MEDICATION

Treatment

Gonorrhoea can be treated with antibiotics.

Medication

Antibiotics Stops the growth or kills bacteria
