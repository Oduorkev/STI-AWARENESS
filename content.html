SYPHILIS

A bacterial infection usually spread by sexual contact that starts
as a painless sore.Syphilis develops in stages and symptoms vary with each stage.




Herpes

Infection with herpes simplex virus (HSV), known as herpes,
is common globally. HSV type 1 (HSV-1) is typically transmitted by
oral-to-oral contact and causes infection in or around the mouth
(oral herpes), but it can also cause genital herpes.
HSV-2 is mainly sexually transmitted and causes genital herpes.




Gonorrhea

Gonorrhea is a sexually transmitted disease (STD) caused by
infection with the Neisseria gonorrhoeae bacterium




CHLAMYDIA

A common, sexually transmitted infection that may not cause symptoms.
Chlamydia affects people of all ages but is most common in young women.
